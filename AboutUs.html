Who are we?

Made with heart and soul, Tipsy Topsy Eggy is more than just a restaurant. It’s a labor of love from a group of passionate individuals. Our team is made up of Ruby, Bora, Zett, Main, Ouu Nhout, Kyat Ou, Day, DKSN, Kore, Ruto, Do Do, and Nora. We’re not just business partners; we’re university classmates who bonded over late-night study sessions, shared laughter, and a mutual love for food and drink. Our journey began with a simple idea: why not create a space where we could share our favorite flavors and good vibes with the world? That’s how Tipsy Topsy Eggy came to life.

Our Vision

Empathy is our compass, and respect is our standard. We believe in the power of community, and that’s why we’ve built our restaurant around the idea of connection between friends, family, and even strangers. Every dish we serve is a reflection of our diverse tastes and backgrounds, crafted with the utmost care to deliver the best flavors you’ve ever tasted. From the spices we source to the local produce we handpick, quality is at the heart of everything we do.

What we offer

At Tipsy Topsy Eggy, we specialize in more than just food. We create experiences. Whether you’re here to enjoy a hearty brunch with friends, unwind after a long day with a perfectly crafted cocktail, or simply savor a quiet moment with a cup of our signature cocktail, we’ve got something for everyone. Our menu is a blend of comfort classics and innovative new dishes, all designed to delight your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Our atmosphere

We know that our guests come here to relax and unwind, and that’s why we’ve paid special attention to creating a fresh, clean, and inviting atmosphere. From the cozy seating to the ambient lighting, every detail is designed to make you feel at home. Our space is perfect for everything from casual meetups to special celebrations, and our friendly staff is always ready to make your visit memorable.

Why Choose us?

When you choose Tipsy Topsy Eggy, you’re not just choosing a place to eat. You’re choosing an experience. We’ve taken care of every detail so that all you have to do is sit back, relax, and enjoy. Whether you’re here for the food, the drinks, or the good company, you’ll leave feeling refreshed and inspired.
